About

I may work in payments, but I’m a people-first kind of guy.

As Director of Sales at Equinox Payments, I help ISVs and ISOs scale with seamless hardware integrations and long-term solutions that drive growth. I bring a consultative approach shaped by years in the field, including over a decade of hands-on experience with Equinox products—even before joining the team.

My path here wasn’t traditional. I started as a barista and DJ, managed teams early on, and studied business and broadcasting in college. Along the way, I learned that listening matters more than talking, strong teams beat solo talent, and growth comes from staying just a little uncomfortable.

I’m big on emerging tech, passionate about supporting software partners, and always focused on building real relationships—whether with clients, teammates, or partners.

When I’m not working, I’m either watching the Lakers, DJing, or running around with my two kids.

Let’s connect to talk payments strategy, ISV growth, or just swap leadership lessons from the field.
