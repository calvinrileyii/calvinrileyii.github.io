About

A partnerships operator who actually builds the AI

I'm Calvin Riley — a product-partnerships operator with 15+ years leading complex product and technology partnerships end-to-end at commerce and payments companies. I source, evaluate, negotiate, and secure partnerships aligned with company priorities, structure agreements that balance partner needs with product objectives, and own the integration experience all the way through measured adoption. I'm trusted across C-suite, product, engineering, finance, and legal — the connective tissue that gets cross-functional deals done.

I now turn that operator's instinct toward building AI products. As an MBA candidate in Applied AI at Northwest University, I research the friction, decompose the workflow, design the agent, and validate it with synthetic personas and real testers before shipping. I move fast, pressure-test ideas against real-world viability, and care more about shipping something that works than sounding impressive. The portfolio below is the proof of work.
